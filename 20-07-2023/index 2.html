SAYLANI WELFARE TRUST

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a non-government organization (NGO) focusing primarily on feeding the poor and homeless

Saylani Welfare International Trust is one of the leading NGOs in Pakistan, established in May 1999 by famous spiritual and religious scholar Moulana Muhammad Bashir Farooqui

Its purpose is to break the cycle of poverty, alleviate the financial troubles of the poor, and give people a chance to live a dignified life.
